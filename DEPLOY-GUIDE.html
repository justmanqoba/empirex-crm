ü™ú Deployment Steps
Step 1
Push files to GitHub
Go to github.com and sign in to your account.

Click the green "New" button to create a new repository.

Name it:
empirex-crm
Set it to Public, then click "Create repository".

On the next screen, click "uploading an existing file", then drag and drop ALL 3 files from this folder (index.html, netlify.toml, DEPLOY-GUIDE.html) into the upload area.

Scroll down and click "Commit changes".
üí° You only need to do this once. Future updates are just drag-and-drop.
Step 2
Create a free Netlify account
Go to netlify.com and click "Sign up".

Choose "Sign up with GitHub" ‚Äî this connects your GitHub so Netlify can read your files.

Authorise Netlify to access your GitHub account when prompted.
Step 3
Deploy your site
Once logged into Netlify, click "Add new site" ‚Üí "Import an existing project".

Choose GitHub, find your empirex-crm repository and select it.

Leave all settings as default and click "Deploy site".

Wait about 30 seconds. Netlify will give you a URL like:
https://random-name-123.netlify.app
Step 4
Set your custom subdomain
In your Netlify dashboard, go to Site configuration ‚Üí Site details ‚Üí "Change site name".

Type your preferred name:
empirex-crm
Your app will then be live at:
https://empirex-crm.netlify.app
Share this link with your clients ‚Äî they can open it on any phone or computer.
‚ö†Ô∏è If empirex-crm is taken, try empirexlegacy-crm or empirex-hub
